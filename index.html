Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions our patients ask about our services and policies.

What are your visiting hours for patients?
Our visiting hours are from 9 AM to 7 PM daily, allowing family and friends to support their loved ones comfortably.
Do you accept insurance and which providers?
We accept most major insurance providers. Please contact our billing department for detailed information about your specific plan.
How can I schedule an appointment?
Appointments can be scheduled online through our website or by calling our reception desk during business hours.
What should I bring for my first visit?
Please bring a valid ID, your insurance card, a list of current medications, and any relevant medical records.
Do you offer emergency services?
Yes, our emergency department is open 24/7, staffed with experienced medical professionals ready to assist you.
